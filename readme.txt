== Description ==

ProMotion Lite is a modern, high-performance WooCommerce theme built exclusively for electronics and gadget stores. Designed to highlight cutting-edge products and irresistible deals, it delivers a powerful shopping experience that converts visitors into loyal customers.
Whether you're selling smartphones, laptops, accessories, or smart devices, ProMotion Lite gives your store a sleek, tech-focused look with layouts that showcase products in the most engaging way.

== Privacy ==

ProMotion Lite theme does not collect, store, or share any personal user data by default.

This theme does not include any tracking mechanisms, analytics scripts, or external service integrations that collect user data without user consent.

If the site owner chooses to integrate third-party services (such as analytics tools, marketing scripts, or external APIs), those services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Any optional integrations that may involve data collection must be explicitly enabled by the user (opt-in). No data collection occurs automatically upon theme activation.

It is the responsibility of the site owner to ensure compliance with applicable privacy laws and to provide a privacy policy if required.
